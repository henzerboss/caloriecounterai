2. Information We Collect

We collect and process only the information needed to provide, operate, and improve the App.

2.1. Information Stored Locally on Your Device

Most of your personal data is stored locally on your device and is not stored on our own servers.

This may include:

Personal Profile Data: age, weight, height, gender, activity level, and nutrition goals.
Food Diary Entries: food names, nutritional information, quantities, meal history, and diary notes.
Streak and Progress Data: completed diary days, missed days, current streak, best streak, and related progress markers.
Statistics Data: consumed calories, nutrients, daily totals, weekly totals, monthly totals, and related progress information.
App Settings: language, theme, notification preferences, unit system, Health integration status, and other local preferences.
Local Backups: backup files created by you through the App, if you use this feature.
2.2. Photos, Text, and Audio Used for Food Analysis

When you actively use food analysis features, the App may process:

photos you take with the camera;
images you select from your gallery;
text descriptions of food;
short audio recordings or transcribed food descriptions, if you use voice input.

These inputs are used only to provide the requested food recognition or nutrition analysis feature.

We do not intentionally use your food photos, audio recordings, or food descriptions for advertising or tracking.

2.3. Health and Fitness Data from Apple Health and Health Connect

If you choose to connect Apple Health on iOS or Health Connect on Android, the App may request permission to read and/or write certain health and fitness data.

This integration is optional. The App will continue to work if you do not connect Apple Health or Health Connect.

Depending on your platform and the permissions you grant, the App may process the following health and fitness data:

Height
Weight
Date of birth or age, where available
Total calories burned
Nutrition data, including consumed calories, protein, fat, and carbohydrates

The App may use this data to:

prefill your profile during onboarding;
update your profile if your height or weight changes in Apple Health or Health Connect;
calculate calorie and nutrition goals;
display total burned calories in daily progress and statistics;
show calorie balance between consumed calories and total calories burned;
write consumed calories and nutrients after you add food entries;
update or delete synced nutrition records when you edit or delete food entries in the App;
write updated height and weight when you change these values in the App.

Health and fitness data from Apple Health or Health Connect is used only for App functionality. We do not sell this data, use it for advertising, share it with advertising networks, or use it for cross-app tracking.

You can grant, deny, or revoke access to Apple Health or Health Connect data at any time through your device settings.

2.4. Analytics Data

We use analytics to understand how the App is used and to improve the product. Analytics may include:

screen views;
interactions with features such as adding food, opening premium screens, using streaks, sharing diary summaries, changing periods in statistics, or selecting diary dates;
general technical information such as device type, operating system, app version, language, and approximate usage patterns;
anonymous or pseudonymous identifiers generated by analytics providers.

We do not intentionally send your food diary contents, food names, photos, audio recordings, personal profile values, Apple Health data, or Health Connect data as analytics event parameters.

2.5. Purchase and Subscription Data

If you purchase a subscription or premium feature, the transaction is processed by Apple App Store or Google Play. We may use a third-party subscription management provider to validate and manage subscription status.
